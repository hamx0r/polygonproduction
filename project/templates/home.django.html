About Polygon

Polygon Production has been established since 1979 in Toronto, Canada; originally as a retail provider for leading brands of audio and visual products. Since then, our expertise has expanded to include karaoke and studio design, audio-visual system rentals, backstage technical support, and professional repair services.

For the past 25 years, we have served myriad clients in Canada, China, Dominican Repblic, and Hong Kong.

Our dedicated team of friendly, reliable, and professional consultants are happy to assist you anytime, anywhere. Please do not hesitate to contact usshould you be interested in learning more about our products and services.

  • Studio Setup
  • Audio & Video
  • Karaoke Setup
  • Stage & Booth Setup
  • CCTV & Intercom
  • Sales & Rental Services
