Karaoke and Studio Design

For 25 years, our team of dedicated professionals has served as expert consultants to a wide range of clients in North America. We have provided expert opinions and on-site technical support to numerous radio and television stations, such as ABC, CBC, CBS, CFTO, CTV, City TV, TV Ontario, and CHIN Radio.

We are pleased to share the experience of some of our key projects with you:

  • 1983: Our staff played a prominent role in the design and implementation of the studios at the WCCO TV Station (Minneapolis, USA) from the beginning until it was fully operational
  • 1986: We spent 2 months re-designing and implementing a state-of-the-art studio at the NBC (New York, USA)
  • 1987: We provided engineering and technical support to the Crossroads Christian Communications Inc. (Toronto, Canada) for seven consecutive years
  • 1992: One of the largest karaoke and disco entertainment facility (Apollo) was established in Vancouver (British Columbia, Canada). We were proud providers and designers of the entire audio-visual-lighting system, as well as the computerized karaoke system
  • 1994: We modified and re-designed the sound system for the Civic Centre in the city of Syracuse (New York, USA)
  • 1994: One of the first disco and karaoke entertainment facilities were established in the Dominican Republic, and we were the proud provider of the audio, lighting, wireless computer systems
